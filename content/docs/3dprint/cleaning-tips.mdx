# Cleaning & Surface Care

Our 3D prints are crafted using high-quality PEI build plates to achieve stunning finishes, such as starry rainbow and holographic textures. Because of the nature of these materials, proper maintenance is key to keeping them looking brand new.

## The "Faded" Finish Myth

<Callout type="info" title="It's probably just fingerprints!">
  If your print looks like the finish is wearing off, dulling, or losing its "sparkle," **don't panic.** This is almost always caused by skin oils transferring from your fingers to the print surface.
</Callout>

These oils create a thin, hazy film that hides the intricate texture of the finish. It does not mean your print is damaged; it simply needs a quick clean.

## How to Restore the Shine

To bring back the original finish, follow these simple steps:

1.  **Use Isopropyl Alcohol (IPA):** Apply a small amount of 70% or higher Isopropyl Alcohol to a clean, lint-free microfiber cloth.
2.  **Gently Wipe:** Lightly wipe the affected area. You don't need to scrub hard—the alcohol will break down the skin oils almost instantly.
3.  **Air Dry:** Let the surface air dry for a few seconds. The finish should return to its original, vibrant state immediately.

---

## General Care Tips

*   **Avoid Harsh Chemicals:** Do not use abrasive cleaners, bleach, or acetone on your prints, as these can chemically damage the plastic and ruin the surface texture permanently.
*   **Store Properly:** If you aren't using your keychain for an extended period, keeping it away from direct, high-intensity sunlight can help prevent potential long-term UV degradation of the material.
*   **Handle with Care:** While our materials are durable, avoiding sharp objects will keep that smooth or textured finish scratch-free.
